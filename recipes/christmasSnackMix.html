Christmas snack mix

This Christmas snack is so pretty in a decorative glass container to give to friends, sitters, paper carriers, music teachers, or co-workers during the holidays.

Ingredients

  • 2 (14 ounce) packages red and green candy-coated chocolate
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar dry roasted peanuts
  • 1 (14 ounce) package chocolate covered peanuts
  • 1 (7 ounce) jar wheat nuts

Directions

  1. Mix together red and green candy-coated chocolate pieces, peanuts, chocolate-covered peanuts, and wheat nuts in a large bowl until combined.
  2. Serve in a large bowl or place in decorative glass jars and give as gifts.
Recipe by: Cindy Carnes
